Appendix C — Troubleshooting Quick Reference

C.1 Engine Won't Start

SymptomLikely CauseCheck / Action
Starter doesn't turn — no soundDead battery, loose/corroded connection, neutral safety switchCheck battery voltage, clean terminals, try in neutral
Starter clicks but doesn't turnLow battery, bad solenoid, seized engineJump start or charge, check engine oil, try to bar engine over by hand
Starter turns slowlyWeak battery, high resistance connections, bad starterVoltage drop test, clean grounds, load test battery
Engine cranks but no startFuel, air, or compression issueCheck fuel level, fuel filter, air filter, glow plugs (cold start)
White smoke when cranking, no startFuel not reaching cylinders, low compressionPrime fuel system, check for air in fuel lines
Starts then dies immediatelyFuel restriction, air in fuel, injection pump issueCheck fuel supply, bleed air from system

C.2 Engine Performance Issues

SymptomLikely CauseCheck / Action
Lack of power / sluggishClogged air filter, bad fuel, turbo issue, fuel restrictionCheck air filter restriction gauge, inspect turbo, replace fuel filters
Excessive black smokeOver-fueling, restricted air intake, bad injectorsCheck air filter, check boost pressure, injector balance test
Excessive white smoke (warm engine)Coolant in combustion chamber (blown head gasket), injector timingCheck coolant level and for bubbles in radiator, compression test
Excessive blue smokeOil burning — worn piston rings, valve guides, turbo sealsCheck oil consumption, compression test, inspect turbo for oil leaks
Engine overheatingLow coolant, stuck thermostat, clogged radiator, bad fan clutchCheck coolant level, feel radiator (should be hot across), check fan operation
Engine running hot but not overheatingWrong thermostat, gauge error, air in cooling systemVerify with infrared thermometer, burp cooling system
Knocking noise from engineBad injector, rod bearing wear, valve train issueIsolate cylinder with stethoscope, injector balance test, check oil pressure

C.3 Brake System

SymptomLikely CauseCheck / Action
Low air pressure / won't buildCompressor issue, governor issue, major air leakCheck compressor drive, governor cut-out setting, listen for leaks
Air pressure drops quickly when parkedLeak in brake systemSoap test all fittings, chambers, valves. Check gladhands.
Brake pedal feels spongyAir in hydraulic system (if equipped), low fluid, worn liningsBleed brakes, check fluid, check lining thickness
Truck pulls to one side when brakingUneven brake adjustment, contaminated lining, seized caliperCheck pushrod stroke both sides, inspect linings, check caliper slides
Brake squeal or squeakWorn linings, glazed drums, lack of anti-squeal compoundInspect lining and drum condition, apply anti-squeal to pad backing
Brake grab / jerky brakingGrease/oil on lining, out-of-round drum, bad brake hose (collapsing)Inspect lining for contamination, measure drum roundness

C.4 Electrical System

SymptomLikely CauseCheck / Action
Battery goes dead overnightParasitic draw, bad alternator diodeParasitic draw test, alternator diode test
Headlights dim at idle, bright at RPMWeak alternator, bad voltage regulator, loose beltAlternator output test, belt tension check
Warning lights flickeringLoose connection, bad alternatorCheck chassis grounds, alternator output
Turn signal blinks fast (hyper-flash)Burned-out bulb on that circuitCheck and replace bulb. If LEDs, may need resistor.

C.5 Air Conditioning

SymptomLikely CauseCheck / Action
No cold air — compressor not engagingLow refrigerant, bad relay, blown fuse, clutch gap too largeCheck fuse/relay, short-cycle clutch to test, check pressure
Warm air — compressor cycles brieflyLow refrigerant (leak) or bad expansion valveCheck pressures, UV dye test for leaks
Weak airflowBlower motor resistor, clogged cabin filterCheck blower speeds, replace cabin filter

Appendix D — Glossary of Terms

TermDefinition
API AdapterAmerican Petroleum Institute standard bottom-loading adapter — the connection point for loading arms at a terminal.
Bendix DriveThe mechanism on a starter motor that engages the pinion gear with the engine's flywheel ring gear during cranking.
Camlock CouplingA quick-connect hose coupling using pivoting cam arms that lock over a mating flange. Standard for top-loading transfer hoses.
CFR / Code of Federal RegulationsU.S. federal regulations (often referenced for HAZMAT and trucking compliance standards).
CR / Certificate of RegistrationThe official government document proving vehicle registration (LTO in the Philippines).
DOT / Department of TransportationGovernment agency regulating commercial vehicle safety standards. DOT-compliant means meeting minimum safety requirements.
Dead-blow HammerA hammer filled with shot or sand that delivers force without rebound — prevents marring surfaces and reduces user fatigue.
Dielectric GreaseNon-conductive silicone grease used on electrical connections to prevent corrosion and moisture ingress.
DifferentialThe gear assembly that allows the left and right wheels to rotate at different speeds while receiving power from the driveshaft (necessary for turning).
DOST / Department of Science and TechnologyPhilippine government agency that certifies tank compartment calibration and weighing equipment.
Drain Plug GasketThe crush washer or O-ring that seals the oil drain plug to the oil pan. Should be replaced at every oil change.
ERC 20Emergency Response Guidebook — the guide for first responders to hazardous material incidents.
GHS / Globally Harmonized SystemInternational standard for chemical hazard classification and labeling (pictograms, signal words, hazard statements).
GladhandThe quick-connect coupling for air brake hoses between tractor and trailer (or between truck sections).
Governor (Air Brake)The device that controls when the air compressor loads (builds pressure) and unloads (idles), maintaining system pressure between cut-in and cut-out.
GVW / Gross Vehicle WeightThe total weight of the truck including chassis, body, payload, fuel, and occupants.
HAZMAT / Hazardous MaterialsDangerous goods that require special handling, placarding, and documentation during transport (e.g., petroleum products).
HEPA / High-Efficiency Particulate AirA filtration standard that captures at least 99.97% of particles 0.3 microns — used for vacuuming brake dust.
Interval ValueThe numeric value in the PM schedule that determines when a task becomes due, combined with the interval unit (km, hrs, days).
JO / Job OrderA digital work order in the system that records: truck, tasks, parts used, labor hours, and completion status.
KPI / Key Performance IndicatorA measurable value that tracks performance — in this system: jobs completed, hours worked, parts cost, etc.
LEL / Lower Explosive LimitThe minimum concentration of a flammable gas or vapor in air that can ignite. Used in confined space and tank entry monitoring.
LOTO / Lockout-TagoutA safety procedure that ensures equipment is isolated from all energy sources and locked to prevent accidental re-energization during maintenance.
LTO / Land Transportation OfficePhilippine government agency responsible for vehicle registration and driver licensing.
ManlidThe hinged cover on top of each tank compartment through which product is loaded (top-loaded trucks) or inspected.
NIOSH / National Institute for Occupational Safety and HealthU.S. agency that certifies respirators and other safety equipment (N95, N100, P100 ratings).
OR / Official ReceiptThe proof of payment for vehicle registration renewal (LTO), carried together with the CR.
Overfill PreventionA safety system that automatically stops product flow when a tank compartment reaches its maximum fill level.
P/V Vent / Pressure-Vacuum VentA valve on top of the tank that allows air to enter (when product is being pumped out) and allows vapors to exit (if pressure exceeds set point), preventing tank collapse or overpressure.
PASS MethodFire extinguisher operation sequence: Pull the pin, Aim at the base, Squeeze the handle, Sweep side to side.
PM / Preventive MaintenanceScheduled, proactive maintenance performed to prevent equipment failure before it occurs.
PMVI / Private Motor Vehicle InspectionAnnual mandatory vehicle roadworthiness inspection in the Philippines (LTO-accredited).
PPE / Personal Protective EquipmentSafety equipment worn to protect against workplace hazards: safety glasses, gloves, steel-toe boots, etc.
Pushrod StrokeThe distance the brake chamber pushrod travels when brakes are applied. Measured to determine if the slack adjuster needs adjustment.
QC / Quality ControlThe inspection process performed before returning a truck to service after any repair or PM.
SAE / Society of Automotive EngineersStandards organization that defines oil viscosity grades (e.g., SAE 15W-40) and fastener specifications.
SDS / Safety Data SheetA document that lists the properties, hazards, handling, and emergency procedures for a chemical product. Must be available for every chemical in the workshop.
Slack AdjusterA lever arm on the brake camshaft that converts the pushrod's linear motion into rotary motion to apply the brake shoes. Can be manual or automatic.
Spring BrakeA parking/emergency brake that uses a powerful spring to apply the brakes mechanically when air pressure is released (fall-safe design).
Torque SealA colored paint or lacquer applied to a tightened fastener to visually indicate that it has been torqued and has not moved.
U-Joint / Universal JointA flexible coupling that allows the driveshaft to transmit power at varying angles (necessary for suspension movement).
UN NumberA four-digit number that identifies a hazardous substance (e.g., UN 1203 = Gasoline, UN 1993 = Diesel/Fuel Oil). Used on placards and transport documents.
Vapor RecoveryA system that captures fuel vapors displaced during loading and returns them to the terminal, preventing vapor release to the atmosphere.
Water Brake (Retarder)A supplemental braking system that uses hydraulic resistance to slow the vehicle, preserving the service brakes for emergency stops. Common on heavy trucks in mountainous terrain.
WeasyPrintA Python library that converts HTML+CSS into PDF documents. Used to generate this SOP manual as a downloadable PDF.
